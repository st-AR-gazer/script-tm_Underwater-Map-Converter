# Moods and Water Color

Water visuals are not only "a water material".

The environments also contain mood-level tuning that affects the look and atmosphere of water.

## Mood settings

Observed mood XML files include water-related parameters such as:

- `WaterFog`

This suggests that mood/atmosphere tuning is part of the water presentation layer.

## `WaterColor.tga`

Some environments also ship a `WaterColor.tga` under mood folders.

Observed examples during the research included:

- BlueBay
- GreenCoast
- RedIsland

This was not observed everywhere in the same way.

## Practical meaning

This matters because:

- even if the block and prefab side is correct
- the final look can still depend on environment-level tuning
