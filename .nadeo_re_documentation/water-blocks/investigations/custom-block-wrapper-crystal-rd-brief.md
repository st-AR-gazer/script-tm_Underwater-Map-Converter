# Custom Block Wrapper / Crystal R&D Brief

Use this as a self-contained prompt for a focused wrapper research session.

## Mission

Investigate custom `.Block.Gbx` wrappers in TM2020, especially:

- `CGameItemModel`
- `CGameBlockItem`
- `CustomizedVariants`
- `CPlugCrystal`

## Practical goal

Determine whether wrappers can do more than just point at an existing block archetype, especially for:

- custom visuals
- lower-clutter water carriers
- stock-client-compatible experiments

## Main questions

- What is the real role of `CustomizedVariants`?
- What is the real role of `Crystal`?
- Why do some wrappers work while others crash?
- Can wrappers help carry custom visuals without custom environment installation?

## Deliverables

- a clear model of wrapper structure
- a clear model of what `Crystal` might be able to do
- the highest-value next experiments
